About
GalaneGroup

Galane Group (Pty) Ltd is a leading provider of integrated solutions in landscaping, cleaning materials, and cleaning equipment. Established in 2020, our company is committed to delivering high-quality products and services that enhance the aesthetics, functionality, and cleanliness of residential, commercial, and industrial environments. Our mission is to transform and maintain outdoor and indoor environments through innovative landscaping designs, premium cleaning materials, and state-of-the-art cleaning equipment. We are dedicated to providing exceptional service and products that meet the highest standards of quality, efficiency, and environmental responsibility.
